making omelettes!

(this should be enough for 2 omelettes)


ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 4 small onions
  • 1 tomato
  • 4 green chilies
  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil
  • a pinch of salt
  • a sprig of curry leaves

instructions:

  1. crack the eggs and beat them in a bowl
  2. cut the onions into small pieces and mix them in
  3. cut the tomato into small pieces and mix them in
  4. cut the green chilies along the width and mix them in
  5. cut the curry leaves into small strips and mix them in with the salt
  6. turn the stove to 6 and put the oil in the pan
  7. pour half of your mixture into the pan
  8. put a cover on the pan
  9. flip the omelette when one side is cooked
  10. take the omelette off when it's fully cooked
  11. repeat steps 7 to 10 for the rest of the mixture

review from adult:

presentation: 9/10, would have looked better with more colors

taste: 8/10, needed more salt

our dog also loved his version (without any spices or other things, just egg)!
