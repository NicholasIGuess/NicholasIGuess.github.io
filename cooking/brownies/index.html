making brownies!

(this should make about 12 to 15 brownies)


ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 38g chocolate chips
  • 133g granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 27g cocoa powder
  • 42g all purpose flour
  • a pinch of salt

instructions:

  1. put aluminum foil in an 8" baking pan
  2. melt the butter and half of the chocolate
  3. mix in the sugar, eggs, and vanilla
  4. mix in the rest of the ingredients
  5. pour into pan and bake for about 30 minutes at 350 degrees
  6. put on wire rack to cool and cut into pieces

review from adult:

presentation: 1/10, looked really good

taste: 9/10, was a bit too hard at first
