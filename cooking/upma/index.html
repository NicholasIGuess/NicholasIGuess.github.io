making upma!

(this should be enough for 3 people)


ingredients:

  • 1 onion
  • 3 cups of vermicelli
  • 6 green chillies
  • 1.5 cups of mixed vegetables
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of mustard seeds
  • 2 teaspoons of urad dal
  • 1.5 teaspoons of salt
  • roasted peanuts to garnish

instructions:

  1. slit the green chilies and cut the onions
  2. add the oil, urad dal, mustard seeds, chilies, and onions when the pot is hot
  3. add the frozen vegetables after a minute of sauteing
  4. add water after 2 minutes and put the lid until the water boils
  5. add the vermicelli and salt
  6. wait for all the water to boil away
  7. garnish with roasted peanuts

review from adult:

presentation: 8/10, needed more garnishes, and could have had a side dish like chutney

taste: 7/10, needed more salt
